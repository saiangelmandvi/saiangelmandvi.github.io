About Us

Welcome to the Ahmedabad Tinkering Community, where we empower young minds to explore, innovate, and create. Founded by Dhruv Saidava, our community is dedicated to fostering a culture of hands-on learning and creativity among students and enthusiasts in Ahmedabad.

Our Programs and Activities

  • Workshops: Participate in hands-on workshops on various topics like robotics, coding, and 3D printing.
  • Hackathons and Competitions: Showcase innovations in local and national competitions.
  • Community Projects: Collaborate on projects addressing real-world challenges.

Mentorship and Support

Our mentors are here to support and guide your child throughout their tinkering journey. With their expertise and experience, your child will receive personalized guidance, helping them turn their ideas into reality.
